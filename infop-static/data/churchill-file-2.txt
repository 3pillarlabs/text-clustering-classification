data/churchill-file-2.txt||||As prime minister, Sir Winston Churchill rallied the British people during WWII, and led his country from the brink of defeat to victory. Born to an aristocratic family in 1874, Winston served in the British military and worked as a writer before going into politics. After becoming prime minister in 1940, he helped lead a successful Allied strategy with the U.S. and Russia during WWII to defeat the Axis powers and craft post-war peace. Elected prime minister again in 1951, he introduced key domestic reforms. Churchill died at age 90 in 1965.
